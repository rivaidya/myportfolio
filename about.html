Currently I am a masters student at School of Information at University of Michigan. I am endlessly curious user experience designer. I speak for user needs through researching, designing and implementing an output that reflects strong understanding of UX principles.


Born and raised in Mumbai, India. I graduated from University of Mumbai in 2014 with B.E. in Instrumentation Engineering. At the same time, I was studying German language and culture. Later in 2016, I completed my masters in business administration from Indo-German Training Center in Mumbai. I draw inspiration from my background as a control system engineer, and student of business and culture to bring empathy and human-centered approach to my work.


Outside the user experince world, I am a yogi, an avid reader and tea lover. I am obsessed with details and never go anywhere without my yellow notebook
